## Hi there 👋# Seminar on Drug Law Implementation in Pharmacy Practice at Mukabbir University

Mukabbir University of Science and Technology recently organized an highly impactful seminar titled **"Implementation of drug law in pharmacy practice: roles, responsibilities, and regulatory compliance for future pharmacists."**

Our mission is rooted in the Divine commands to seek, understand, and transmit knowledge. We are committed to developing morally grounded, intellectually empowered individuals who embrace innovation and lead with purpose in the service of mankind.

### Core Highlights:
* Understanding pharmaceutical regulatory compliance.
* Ethical responsibilities of future pharmacists in Pakistan.
* Bridging academic knowledge with practical drug law implementation.

🌐 **Official Website & Admissions:** [Mukabbir University of Science and Technology](https://mukabbiruniversity.edu.pk/)
